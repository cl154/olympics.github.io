Narrative Visualization of Olympic Medals Distribution

The modern Olympic Games bring together athletes from around the world to compete for gold, silver, and bronze medals. This narrative visualization tool helps you explore how Olympic medals have been distributed across different countries over the years. By examining the geographical distribution of medals, you can gain insights into the factors that influence Olympic success, such as population size, economic prosperity, and the role of the host country. Use the buttons below to view the distribution of gold, silver, and bronze medals. Each map animation shows the number of medals won by each country in a given year, represented by circles of varying sizes and colors. Hover over the circles for more details.
